Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist. He developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics. Einstein's work is also known for its influence on the philosophy of science. Wikipedia Born: 14 March 1879, Ulm, Germany Died: 18 April 1955, Princeton, New Jersey, United States Spouse: Elsa Einstein (m. 1919–1936), Mileva Marić (m. 1903–1919) Children: Eduard Einstein, Lieserl Einstein, Hans Albert Einstein
